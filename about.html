I am a graphic designer based in Auckland/ New Zealand. At the moment I am working with B2B and marketing projects for a software company. My way to become who I am has been very colourful and adventures. I started as a textile designer with a Bachelor in my home land Latvia. The individual way of drawing, I discovered while studying Illustration at a very creative studio of ASP in Warsaw/ Poland. However, the conceptual thinking I developed while doing Master of Graphic design in Netherlands. I love travelling, I love rain in hot days and smell of lilac. I love to work with hands, I love qulity. I love to be honest and natural. I am a firm believer to make this world a better place.
