ADD YOUR IMAGINATION TO THE MUSEUM

The public museums started in the middle of 18th century and they have been changed since then. Despite that many people have the same feeling about museum - it is boring, cold, old fashion and never changes. Majority don’t go to museums, because they don’t want to feel stupid, never had interest or thought they will not understand art. But there is no right way how to look at art, there is a freedom of interpretation, you should pick your own way. My goal is to show few ways how to start imagine and interpretate art.

The hand drawing on front of the card shows a famous art peace. Insde the card, there is a photography of an original art work. When you open further, there is a description about an artist and the movement. And at the end - offer to buy a museum card to visit museums for one year.

The card is like a game - to make an imagination be possible. If you don’t open a card by the rules, but see it from other sides, you can see that it is possible to play with the artwork, change order of the fragments and to see an art from the different perspective.
